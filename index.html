Your Report: The Key to Support

What's Inside Your Report?

Your comprehensive report includes a formal diagnosis (where applicable) and clear, practical recommendations for support at work or in education.

  • APC Qualified: Authored by an experienced, fully certified professional.
  • DSA Compliant: Fully meets the requirements for applications to the Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA).
  • Access Arrangements: Recommendations for appropriate Access Arrangements (e.g., extra time in exams).
  • SpLD Investigation: Explicit investigation and consideration of dyslexia, dyspraxia and ADHD as SpLDs.

Unlock Educational Support (DSA)

If you are a student, a compliant report is essential for accessing the Disabled Students’ Allowance.

What DSA Can Cover:

  • Specialist equipment (laptops, software).
  • Non-medical helpers (study skills tutors).
  • Travel and general expenses related to your disability.

Our report ensures you meet the documentary standards needed for this process.
